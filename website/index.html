What it does

A simple tool with a very narrow job.

WhisperNote is for moments when typing is slower than talking. It captures your speech, turns it into text, and keeps that text close at hand so you can copy it, edit it, or move on.

01

Capture

Start with a click or a hotkey. The window stays small, floating, and easy to reach.

02

Transcribe

Your voice becomes text quickly, with the option to use hosted services or local Whisper.

03

Keep

Every completed note is stored locally so you can revisit it later or copy it again.

Inside the app

Built to stay quiet until you need it.

The interface is intentionally small: a mic button, a waveform, and a few controls. That keeps the focus on the note itself instead of the tool.

  • Global hotkey for quick capture
  • Pause, resume, or discard while recording
  • Hosted or local transcription depending on your preference
  • Notes saved in a local database

For non-technical users

No setup maze. No giant dashboard. Just a fast voice note pad.

WhisperNote is useful when you want to speak a thought, capture it immediately, and move on. It is designed to feel like a small utility rather than another app you have to manage.
